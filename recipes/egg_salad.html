Best Potato Salad

This best-ever potato salad is the creamiest, richest potato salad you will ever try. The secret is in the homemade dressing. I get nothing but raves every time I make this.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Remove eggs from hot water; cool, peel, and chop.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place chopped eggs in a bowl; stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper.

  5. Step 5

    6. Stir and serve on your favorite bread, crackers, or salad greens.

    Recipe Tip

    To make hard-boiled eggs, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil, then immediately remove from the heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from the hot water, cool, and peel.
